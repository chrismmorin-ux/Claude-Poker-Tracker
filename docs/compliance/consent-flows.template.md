## How to review

For each flow:

1. **The flow is required by the named jurisdiction(s)** — do you actually serve users covered by that regulation?
2. **The trigger location is correct** — does the consent prompt fire at the right point in the user's lifecycle?
3. **The wording is acceptable** — legal-guardian persona drafted for legal solidity, but each jurisdiction has nuances that may need legal counsel review.
4. **The withdrawal mechanism works** — can a user actually withdraw consent, and does withdrawal produce the right downstream effect (e.g., data deletion or processing-pause)?
5. **The audit log captures the right fields** — timestamp, user_id, consent_version, IP address, jurisdiction, etc.
