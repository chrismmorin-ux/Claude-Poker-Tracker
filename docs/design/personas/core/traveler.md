# Persona — The Traveler

**Type:** Core (end-user archetype)
**Evidence status:** PROTO — unverified
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-21

---

## Snapshot

Plays cash in multiple countries and casinos. Currencies convert, session boundaries cross time zones, rake structures differ, tax reporting spans borders. A subset of the Rounder or Circuit Grinder with multi-currency / multi-venue accounting as the defining constraint.

## Context

- **Venue:** Live, cross-border.
- **Format:** Cash mid-to-high stakes.
- **Skill:** Advanced.
- **Volume:** 80–150 hrs/month.
- **Device:** Phone + laptop.
- **Intent:** Unified tracking across venues + tax-friendly export.
- **Role:** Solo.

## Goals

- Multi-currency bankroll with automatic FX.
- Per-venue win rate comparison (is Vegas worth more than Macau?).
- Visa-friendly and tax-friendly session export (country-specific formats).
- Quickly evaluate "is this new casino worth my time?"

## Frustrations

- Currency handling is usually an afterthought.
- Jet-lagged session boundaries (did that 4am session belong to Tuesday or Wednesday?).
- Casino-specific rake / cap structures ignored.

## Non-goals

- Coaching.
- Drills (already advanced).

---

## Constraints

- **Time pressure:** Full at table, high off-table (booking flights, visa paperwork).
- **Error tolerance:** Zero for financial accuracy.
- **Complexity tolerance:** High.
