# Persona — The Apprentice

**Type:** Core (end-user archetype)
**Evidence status:** PROTO — unverified
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-21

---

## Snapshot

Paying a coach or self-studying with a small friend group. Improving, serious about it, hates being bad. Wants structure — flag hands for review, complete assigned drills, understand *why* a decision was wrong, see objective improvement over time.

## Context

- **Venue:** Online or live, low-to-mid stakes.
- **Format:** Cash or MTT.
- **Skill:** Improving (recreational-to-serious).
- **Volume:** 20–40 hrs/month play + 5–10 hrs/month study.
- **Device:** Phone during play, desktop for study.
- **Intent:** Structured improvement.
- **Role:** Student (may also be an entry-level Rounder).

## Goals

- Flag hands for coach review with one tap mid-session.
- Complete assigned drills between sessions.
- Understand the *reasoning* behind right vs. wrong decisions.
- Track personal skill improvement objectively over time.

## Frustrations

- Lost in analysis jargon (MDF, SPR, EV).
- Coach's feedback comes a week later when the hand is cold.
- Existing tools don't bridge live play and drill study.

## Non-goals

- Self-directed deep research (they have a coach for that).
- Coaching others.

---

## Constraints

- **Time pressure:** Full at the table, low off-table.
- **Error tolerance:** Moderate.
- **Visibility tolerance:** Low initially, growing.
- **Complexity tolerance:** Low to moderate — tolerates jargon if explained.
