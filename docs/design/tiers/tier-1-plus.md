# Tier 1 — Plus / Regular

**ID:** `tier-1-plus`
**Role:** Full individual use for a serious hobbyist
**Target $/mo (placeholder):** ~$19
**Last reviewed:** 2026-04-21

---

## Who this tier is for

- Primary: [Weekend Warrior](../personas/core/weekend-warrior.md) (upgraded), [Scholar](../personas/core/scholar-drills-only.md), [Apprentice](../personas/core/apprentice-student.md), [Ringmaster](../personas/core/ringmaster-home-host.md) (individual)
- Secondary: entry point for [Hybrid](../personas/core/hybrid-semi-pro.md)

## Role vs. adjacent tiers

- Upgrade from: Free (after cap or feature need)
- Upgrade to: Pro (when sidebar, advanced exploit engine, or ICM is needed)
- Reason to upgrade to Pro: online play, multi-tabling, ICM-critical tournaments, deep villain modeling at scale

---

## What's included

### Main app

- Everything in Free, plus:
- Full exploit engine (basic — Layer 1 + Layer 2 depth-1)
- Unlimited tracked players
- Unlimited history
- Multi-device sync (up to 2 devices)
- Hand Replay (view past hands with action replay)
- Full Preflop Drills suite
- Full Postflop Drills (Explorer + Line Study)
- Statistics dashboard
- CSV export
- Player avatar features + visual search
- Basic player analysis / weakness detection

### Sidebar extension

- Not available at this tier.

### Platform-wide

- As Free.

## What's NOT included (moves you to Pro)

- Full Bayesian villain modeling with game-tree EV (depth-3)
- Sidebar extension
- ICM / bounty / satellite features
- Custom drills from own hand history
- Cross-venue player linking
- 3+ device sync

## Caps and limits

- Player database: unlimited
- History window: unlimited
- Device count: 2
- Sync: included (main app)

---

## Typical customer journey

- Weekend Warrior upgrades after hitting Free cap → uses for 2+ months.
- Scholar pays for full drill library → sticks around if daily drill habit forms.
- Apprentice bundled by coach → stays as long as coaching relationship does.
- Ringmaster pays to track their personal game + individual stats.

## Retention risks

- Sync issues across 2 devices frustrate enough to cancel.
- Exploit advice feels same as Free → no perceived value.
- Competitor free tiers erode price differential.
